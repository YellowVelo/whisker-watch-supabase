PRODUCT CONTEXT 
Purpose: Define what Wysker Watch is
Audience: Product, UX, Engineering, AI, Contributors
Product Context
Wysker Watch is a proactive health management platform for cats and dogs. It helps owners observe, understand, and manage their pet’s health through simple, behavior based tracking and unified health information.
Every decision begins here. If a feature does not support this definition, it does not belong in Wysker Watch.
Core Goal
Help pet owners recognize meaningful changes in their pet’s health as early as possible while making their lives simpler—not more complicated.
What Wysker Watch Is
A platform that captures daily observations, organizes health information, and reveals meaningful trends over time. It brings together:
•	Daily observations
•	Medications
•	Nutrition
•	Weight
•	Preventive care
•	Vaccinations
•	Laboratory results
•	Veterinary visits
•	Health records
•	Photos & documents
•	Reminders
•	Long term trends
The platform creates a unified, accessible health timeline for each pet.
Why It’s Needed
Pet health information is scattered across calendars, notes, messages, invoices, and memory. Owners notice small changes but often forget them before veterinary visits. Existing apps focus on isolated tasks; few connect everyday observations into long term understanding.
Wysker Watch makes capturing observations effortless and transforms them into meaningful insight.
Who It’s For
Primary Users
•	Cat owners
•	Dog owners
•	First time pet owners
•	Owners managing chronic conditions
•	Owners focused on preventive care
•	Senior pet owners
Secondary Users
•	Multi pet households
•	Families sharing care
•	Pet sitters
•	Veterinarians reviewing longitudinal history
What Makes Wysker Watch Different
•	Behavior first tracking
•	Individualized baselines
•	Trend based change detection
•	Unified health picture
•	Low friction daily interactions
•	AI assisted understanding (not diagnosis)
Version 1 Scope
•	Pet profiles
•	Daily check ins
•	Catch up check ins
•	Timeline of health events
•	Medication management
•	Preventive care reminders
•	Health records & documents
•	Weight & health trends
•	AI summaries & insights
•	Veterinary reports
•	Multi pet support
Version 1 is not: Practice management software, EMR, telemedicine, diagnostics, wearables, insurance management, prescription ordering, social networking, or multi species support.
Definition of Success
Wysker Watch succeeds when:
•	Daily tracking becomes a natural habit
•	Owners recognize meaningful changes earlier
•	Veterinary appointments become more productive
•	Trends are easy to understand
•	Owners feel more confident managing their pet’s health
