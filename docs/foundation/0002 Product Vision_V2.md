PRODUCT VISION (Final Version)
Purpose: Define why Wysker Watch exists and the future it aims to create
Audience: Product, UX, Engineering, AI, Contributors
Product Vision
The Product Vision defines the long term direction of Wysker Watch. It explains why the product exists and the future it aims to create.
Mission
Help pet owners recognize meaningful changes in their pet’s health through simple, consistent observation—enabling earlier conversations, better decisions, and stronger partnerships with veterinary professionals.
Vision
We envision a future where every pet has a complete health story—not just medical records. A future where subtle changes are recognized earlier, owners feel confident, and veterinarians have richer historical context. A future where proactive care becomes the norm.
Why Wysker Watch Exists
Owners notice small changes every day—appetite shifts, mobility changes, energy fluctuations—but those observations are often forgotten. By the time a veterinary appointment arrives, owners know something has changed but struggle to explain when or how.
Wysker Watch preserves those observations, connects them over time, and transforms them into meaningful understanding.
Not to replace veterinary medicine. To strengthen it.
Philosophy of Pet Healthcare
Healthcare is a partnership.
Veterinarians bring medical expertise. Owners bring daily observation.
A veterinarian may see a pet for minutes. An owner knows that pet for a lifetime.
Wysker Watch strengthens this partnership by preserving accurate, longitudinal history.
Every Pet Deserves a Health Story
Medical records capture appointments. Health stories capture lives.
Medical records begin when something is wrong. Health stories begin long before illness appears.
Wysker Watch preserves that story from the first day a pet comes home through every stage of life.
What We Believe
•	Every pet has its own normal
•	Small changes matter
•	Consistency > perfection
•	Observation > memory
•	Understanding > data collection
•	Simplicity encourages engagement
•	Emotional design supports better care
•	AI should explain, not diagnose
•	Technology should strengthen the owner–veterinarian relationship
•	Better history leads to better care
Five Year Vision
Five years from now, Wysker Watch becomes the most trusted companion for proactive pet health.
Owners shift from asking “What happened?” to “When did this begin?” Veterinarians receive richer histories. Families coordinate care more effectively. Long term patterns become visible. More pets receive earlier intervention.
What Wysker Watch Will Never Become
•	A replacement for veterinarians
•	A diagnostic tool
•	A source of fear or anxiety
•	A burdensome or complex system
•	A data collection engine without purpose
•	A platform that asks owners to become medical professionals
Our Promise
We are preserving the story of a life—helping owners and veterinarians write healthier chapters together.
